Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Darenstefan ("I", "me", "this site") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit darenstefan.com or interact with related pages, forms, and social channels.

Information I Collect

I collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact & story forms: When you submit a form on this site (such as a contact or fan story form), the information you provide — typically your name, email address, and message — is sent to me via Formspree, a third-party form processing service.
  • Email correspondence: If you email me directly (hello@darenstefan.com), I receive and store that correspondence through Zoho Mail.
  • Site analytics: I may use basic, privacy-conscious analytics tools to understand general site traffic (such as page views and referral sources). This data is aggregated and not used to identify you personally.
  • Advertising: If you arrive at this site via a Meta (Facebook/Instagram) ad campaign, Meta may collect data through its own advertising and pixel technologies in accordance with its own privacy policy. I do not control or have direct access to this data beyond aggregated campaign performance metrics.

How I Use Information

Information collected through this site is used only to:

  • Respond to messages, inquiries, and form submissions
  • Understand how people find and use the site, in order to improve it
  • Measure the performance of marketing and advertising campaigns

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

This site relies on a small number of trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Netlify — website hosting
  • Formspree — contact and story form processing
  • Zoho Mail — email hosting and correspondence
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — social media and advertising

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data, and I encourage you to review them directly on their respective websites.

Cookies

This site may use minimal cookies or similar technologies required for basic functionality and, where applicable, anonymous analytics. It does not use cookies to build detailed personal profiles for resale or third-party marketing.

Data Retention

Form submissions and email correspondence are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or maintain a reasonable record of communication, after which they may be deleted.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of the personal information I hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, simply contact me using the details below.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy
